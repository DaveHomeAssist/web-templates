Weighted Decision
Scoring Matrix

Evaluate multiple options against weighted criteria. Adjust importance sliders to see scores recalculate in real time. Use for vendor selection, platform choice, or any multi-factor decision.

How to use: Each criterion row shows raw scores (1–10) per option. The weighted score factors in importance. Drag the sliders to change how much each criterion matters — totals update automatically.

Scoring guide

9–10: Exceptional — category leader.

7–8: Strong — meets needs well.

5–6: Adequate — works but with trade-offs.

3–4: Weak — meaningful gaps.

1–2: Poor — significant deficiency.
