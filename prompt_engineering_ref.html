Core principles
01 / Specificity

Vague instructions produce vague results. Every word in a prompt spends tokens — spend them on constraints, not filler.

02 / Positive framing

Tell the model what to do, not just what to avoid. Negative instructions alone leave too much open to interpretation.

03 / Separation

Use XML tags or clear delimiters to separate instructions, context, and examples — especially in long prompts.

04 / Prefill

Starting the assistant turn with a partial response steers format and tone more reliably than instructing it at the top.
